Our Communities Matter

At Cmore Healthcare, we believe that tackling the opioid crisis requires a dedicated and compassionate approach focused on recovery, mental well-being, and community support.

Cmore Healthcare Cares

Cmore Healthcare is deeply committed to the well-being of our community, offering integrated mental and physical health services with a dedicated focus on **ending the opioid crisis**. Our comprehensive treatment options for opioid dependence include:

  • Suboxone Treatment
    We provide care for individuals seeking initial evaluation and ongoing Suboxone treatment. Our goal is to stabilize your health and support your journey toward recovery.
  • Accessible Visits
    Established patients have the flexibility of attending appointments via secure Zoom video conferencing, ensuring care remains convenient and consistent.
  • Integrated Care
    We treat associated mental health conditions often concurrent with substance use disorders, including ADHD, anxiety, and depression, referring to specialty professions when necessary to ensure holistic healing.
